Peanutbutter Sandwich

Simply Tasty

Comfort food with healthy peanut butter that even a toddler will eat

  • Sliced Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Banana
  • Honey
  1. Layout two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on both slices
  3. Slice banana and cover one slice
  4. Drizzle Honey for sweetness
